Little Bunny is a freemium tool aimed at assisting casual seller and buyer to communicate and transact the for sale items such as those found on Craigslist or local classified

How does it work?

1. You post your ad at your favorite listing site, including a link(provided by Little Bunny)
2. Potential buyer clicks the link, views the ad on Little Bunny's friendly site
3. You can manage your offer, confirm appointment etc in one place! All this is completely free, always!

Questions and Answers

Why do I want to use Little Bunny as a casual seller?
  • Spam filter: spammers are getting smarter. By using Little Bunny we shield spammers from you
  • Too much a hassle to find a right time to meet the buyer? We help you with an innovative scheduling solution
  • Have a few items to sell? You will find the seller dashboard which shows a glance of all activities to be very handy!
  • Tired of the no-show buyer? We help you to avoid it by requesting a nominal deposit from buyer(we charge a nominal $1 to cover the credit card transaction fee)
